Hello, I’m Sang-il Bak

M.S. candidate in Physics at Chungbuk National University.

I work on axion physics, gravitational waves, and N-body simulations of dense stellar clusters. My research sits at the intersection of particle physics, gravitational wave astronomy, and computational astrophysics.

This site is a mix of my research and things I enjoy outside of physics. Feel free to look around, and don’t hesitate to reach out if you want to talk about physics or anything else.

📧 sangil.bak@chungbuk.ac.kr
